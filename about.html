About me

Disclaimer: The opinions expressed herein are my own personal opinions and do not represent my employer's view in any way.

TL;DR: I love to work with computers and electronics in any kind and I blog about it at afronski.pl.

Wojtek Gawroński (afronski) is a Software Developer in White Rook. He is a passionate programmer (he would like to call himself a hacker), specialized in web development and distributed systems with over 4 years of professional experience. He strongly believes in Test-Driven Development and eXtreme Programming practices.

He was a Team Leader, started by working with .NET technologies (ASP.NET WebForms, ASP.NET MVC, WCF and Azure). He likes the most working with front-end technologies (with JavaScript and especially the newest ones like WebGL, HTML5 and CSS3). He worked with Python, Ruby and Node.js (and loves the last one). Recently working more with the functional programming paradigms (Erlang and Elixir, Clojure and ClojureScript and also Haskell).

Proven ability to work on own initiative and always eager to learning new stuff (actively works with Coursera, Udacity and Code School). Organized (GTD practitioner), detail-oriented and pragmatic. Not afraid to work at a low level with Raspberry Pi and Arduino.

Moreover, he is passionate about:

  • functional programming,
  • internals of compilers, virtual machines, garbage collectors and JITs,
  • computer graphics and game development,
  • algorithms and data structures
